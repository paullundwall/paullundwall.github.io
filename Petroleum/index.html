Petroleum & Sons - Rules & How to Play

Objective

Build a dominant oil empire by controlling oil-rich territories, managing drilling operations, and distributing oil to consumer nations. Compete or collude with your rival, Standard Oil Corporation.

Game Setup

  • You start with $10,000 and basic equipment
  • 6 territories are available for purchase
  • Each territory has different oil reserves and costs
  • Consumer countries need oil deliveries

Turn Structure

  1. Purchase Phase: Buy oil-rich territories
  2. Drilling Phase: Set up drilling operations (produces oil automatically)
  3. Trade Phase: Export oil to consumer countries
  4. Negotiation Phase: Compete or collude with rival company

Actions

  • Buy Land: Purchase territories with oil reserves
  • Drill Oil: Install drilling equipment (costs money, produces oil automatically)
  • Export: Sell oil to consumer countries for profit
  • Collude: Cooperate with Standard Oil to fix prices (affects next turn pricing)
  • Compete: Launch aggressive market competition against Standard Oil
  • Note: You can only use either Collude OR Compete once per turn

New Features

  • Limited Purchases: Only one territory can be purchased per turn
  • Drill Upgrades: Upgrade drilling sites to increase production by 25% per level (max 3 levels)
  • Dynamic Pricing: All costs, reserves, and prices are randomized each game
  • Historical Setting: Game starts in 1955, each turn represents one year

Winning

Game ends after 10 turns (1955-1964). Win by having the most money and oil reserves combined.
